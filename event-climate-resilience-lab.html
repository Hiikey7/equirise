Climate Resilience Lab

This ongoing lab convenes multidisciplinary teams to strengthen climate adaptation, risk management, and locally-led resilience practices.

Participants co-create resilient systems for agriculture, water, energy and community safety, using evidence-based design and partnership models.

The lab is structured around sector-focused working groups and showcases real-world resilience initiatives from across Africa.

It supports policy implementation, stakeholder collaboration, and adaptive learning for public and private actors.

Resilience emerges when local knowledge, planning and resources come together with strong institutional support.

Grace Osei . Nairobi

The Climate Resilience Lab is tailored for practitioners in government, civil society, finance, and infrastructure delivery.

What to expect

Collaborative sessions, technical clinics, and practical tools for resilience strategy development and implementation.
