Procurement Innovation Workshop

This completed workshop explored procurement systems, digital tools, and public oversight mechanisms that improve accountability.

Participants reviewed procurement innovation case studies, modern contracting approaches, and practical steps for reform.

The workshop underscored the importance of transparency, stakeholder inclusion, and agile procurement governance.

It also highlighted cross-sector collaboration between procurement professionals, service providers, and civil society.

Procurement innovation begins with clearer processes, stronger governance, and better collaboration.

David Achebe . Nairobi

This event was designed for procurement leaders, compliance officers, and partners seeking practical accountability improvements.

Workshop highlights

Practical sessions on procurement design, risk mitigation, supplier engagement, and digital workflows for purchasing teams.
